# TOOLS.md — News

## Source Priority
1. Reuters, AP, Bloomberg
2. Central banks (Fed, ECB, BNM, PBoC)
3. Government policy announcements
4. Energy agencies (IEA, EIA, OPEC)
5. Military/defense statements

## Avoid
- Wikipedia, non-official blogs
- Social media without primary source
- Opinion pieces without factual anchor
- Paywalled content (unless agent-browser)
- Aggregators (Google News, Yahoo Finance)

## Browser
- web_fetch: 90% (quick text)
- agent-browser: JS rendering, paywalls, cookie walls
