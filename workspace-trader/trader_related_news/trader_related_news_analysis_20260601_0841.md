# Trader Analysis: US-Iran Deal Negotiations
**Date/Time:** 2026-06-01 08:41 (Perth)

## Headline
Trump seeking edits to US-Iran deal, US media report

## Summary
President Donald Trump has requested additional amendments to the proposed US-Iran ceasefire framework. The new requested edits focus on the management of the Strait of Hormuz and the removal of highly enriched uranium. These developments come as negotiations remain highly volatile, with Iran's chief negotiator stating that no deal will be reached without full security of Iranian rights, including the release of frozen assets. US Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth has warned that military strikes could resume if the negotiations do not meet the President's expectations.

## Trade Relevance
**HIGH.** The negotiations directly involve the **Strait of Hormuz**, a critical global energy chokepoint. Any instability or failure to reach a deal involving the Strait could lead to significant volatility in global oil and gas prices. Furthermore, the mentions of **highly enriched uranium** and **sanctions relief** (access to frozen assets) have direct implications for geopolitical risk premiums in energy and broader equity markets.
