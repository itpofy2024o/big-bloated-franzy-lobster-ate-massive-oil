Headline: IDF says it has identified missiles fired at Israel from Iran
Summary: The Israel Defense Forces (IDF) identified missiles launched from Iran toward Israel on Sunday. This follows Israeli airstrikes on Hezbollah targets in southern Beirut, Lebanon, over the weekend. Iranian lawmaker Ebrahim Rezaei had previously threatened a "decisive and painful response" to the Beirut strikes. The IDF has issued precautionary directives to residents in targeted areas to seek shelter. This escalation occurs amid a tenuous ceasefire and ongoing U.S.-Iran peace negotiations.
