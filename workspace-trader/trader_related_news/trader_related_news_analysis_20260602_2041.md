## News Analysis: Israel-Hezbollah Escalation in Beirut
**Headline:** Israel PM orders strikes on Beirut suburbs as Hezbollah conflict escalates
**Summary:** Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has ordered military strikes against the Hezbollah-controlled southern suburbs of Beirut (Dahieh). This marks a major escalation in the conflict, signaling a shift from border skirmishes to targeting the core of Hezbollah's influence in the Lebanese capital. The move follows the collapse of a US-brokered ceasefire and is occurring amidst intense diplomatic efforts by the US to mediate.
**Trade Relevance:** HIGH. This escalation increases geopolitical risk in the Middle East, which can lead to immediate volatility in energy markets (oil/gas) and a flight to safe-haven assets like gold and US Treasuries. Additionally, the involvement of advanced drone technology may impact defense sector sentiment.
**Classification:** CRITICAL
